# Stay Safe Online: Checklist for College Students in India

## 1. Password and Account Security
- Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.

## 2. Email and Link Safety
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading attachments from unknown emails or messages.
- Verify the sender before opening links or attachments.

## 3. Device and Software Protection
- Regularly update your devices, apps, and antivirus software.
- Backup important data regularly and encrypt sensitive files when possible.

## 4. Social Media and Personal Information
- Be cautious when sharing personal information online.
- Review your privacy settings regularly.

## 5. Financial/UPI Safety
- Do not share OTPs, UPI PINs, or banking passwords with anyone.
- Verify payment requests or transaction messages before authorizing UPI or online payments.
- Use official apps and websites for online banking and e-commerce transactions.

## 6. Awareness and Reporting
- Educate yourself about common phishing, malware, and online scams.
- Report cybercrimes or suspicious online activity to:
  - National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
  - Helpline: 1930
